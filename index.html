Laundry Day

This was the very first time I'd done any serious kind of video editing. To be honest, I wasn't very confident in how it would turn out, eventhough it wasn't supposed to be the very best being that it was the beginning of the semester, but for what I did I feel that I did a decent job.

This was the first time we were able to experiment with Premier and it was actually a little intimidating at first, I had a lot of trouble with this becuase I was trying to adjust back into schoo life as well as my new device, but it was pretty fun to learn how some of my favorite vloggers and Youtubers do their video editing.

During my course I was required to edit a piece of video and present what it'd be like as an installation. What I did was supposed to be a somewhat personal piece, I am battling with anxiety which often leads me to want to stay inside constantly, so I thought to make a film on what my 'day' usually is like when I pretty much have no courage to leave.

This was my final submission to the course and honestly this was the most fun I'd had while making it. It was of course exhausting to get the footage I needed, sitting for long periods can be a pain but it was very liberating. A lot of the time I don't have much fun playing but when I stream (which is what all the pieces of footage are from) I feel happier cause I like to make people laugh, and nothing makes me laugh more than silly streamers. So I'm really glad that I took this course because I learned a lot about film editing magic.
